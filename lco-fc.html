Our Purpose

At lco.fc, football is a catalyst for unity, athletic excellence, and technological innovation. We aren't just building competitive squads; we are establishing a unique ecosystem where our athletes drive the development of SihatBot. By collecting and analyzing real-time player health and performance data on the pitch, our team serves as the foundational testing ground to pioneer next-generation medical triage AI. Technology is our enabler, but character, passion, and legacy serve as our definitive compass.

Our Leadership

Our core team leads with absolute purpose—steering strategy, analyzing performance, and establishing a dominant blueprint for the academy. We focus on player pathway optimization, physical and technical excellence, and exporting talent to global leagues.
